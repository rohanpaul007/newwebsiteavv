Setting Global Standards for Excellence

At ICVMS, we establish rigorous benchmarks for accreditation in Vedic and Metaphysical sciences, ensuring practitioners and institutions meet the highest levels of integrity, authenticity, and professionalism. Our accreditation signifies a commitment to global excellence.

Individual Accreditation

For individual practitioners and scholars, ICVMS certification is a mark of professional credibility and mastery in their respective fields, recognized globally as a standard of excellence.

    Eligibility Criteria

  • Legally registered educational institution.
  • Curriculum aligned with ICVMS standards
  • Qualified faculty with relevant expertise.
  • Established assessment and student support systems.
  • Commitment to continuous improvement and ethical practice.

Process Overview

1 Application Submission

Complete the online application form and submit required documentation, including curriculum details and faculty credentials.

2 Initial Review

Our review panel assesses the submitted materials for completeness and initial eligibility against ICVMS criteria.

3 On-Site Evaluation (Remote Option)

A comprehensive evaluation is conducted, which may include virtual or physical visits to assess facilities, teaching methodologies, and adherence to standards.

4 Accreditation Decision

Complete the online application form and submit required documentation, including curriculum details and faculty credentials.

5 Annual Renewal

Accreditation requires annual renewal, involving submission of updated information and adherence to ongoing quality assurance protocols.

Individual Certification

For individual practitioners and scholars, ICVMS certification is a mark of professional credibility and mastery in their respective fields, recognized globally as a standard of excellence.

    Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum of X years professional experience or relevant academic qualifications.
  • Demonstrated knowledge and understanding of Vedic/Metaphysical principles.
  • Adherence to the ICVMS Code of Ethics.
  • Established assessment and student support systems.
  • Successfully pass ICVMS certification examinations/assessments.

Process Overview

1 Application & Portfolio Submission

Submit your professional profile, educational background, and any relevant portfolio demonstrating your expertise and experience.

2 Panel Review & Interview

An expert panel reviews your application and may conduct an interview to assess your qualifications and ethical understanding.

3 Assessment & Examination

Candidates may undergo a comprehensive assessment or examination to validate their knowledge and practical skills.

4 Certification Approval

Upon successful completion of all stages, the Certification Board grants ICVMS Individual Certification.

5 Continuous Professional Development

Certified individuals are encouraged to engage in ongoing learning and development to maintain and enhance their expertise.
